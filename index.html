About Me

My name is Steve Rokitka (Rocketkar). I'm a full-stack product designer, meaning I create products using the tools of both a designer and a developer.

My career's always been a multidisciplinary one. I started out as a journalist, learned to build websites, spent a decade as different flavors of designer/front-end developer and rolled those skills together with UX research at Cornell University, design training at Seattle's School of Visual Communications and software development at Code Fellows. I've gone deepest on mobile apps, catalog design and dashboards, along with some great indie publishing, data visualization and brand/identity projects.

When I'm not building parts of the internet, I like to go on adventures that require me to learn new things. In the last couple years I've painted a house, rode a motorcycle to Burning Man, got Wilderness First Responder certified, spent six months learning to fix cars, and continued my training in various martial arts.

Today, I'm looking for a new full-time project that'll let me apply what I've learned in new and interesting ways. If you've got something that needs solutions found, ideas given form, stories discovered or just a healthy dose of snark injected into your slack channels, drop me a line and let's get started.
